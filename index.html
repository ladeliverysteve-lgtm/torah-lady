About Dr. Judy

She makes Torah
make sense.

For more than three decades, Dr. Judy Freier has been helping people discover that the Torah isn't a relic — it's a remarkably practical guide to the questions we're all wrestling with right now.

Known affectionately as "The Torah Lady," she has a rare gift for translating ancient text into the language of everyday life: relationships, identity, purpose, loss, and joy. Her approach is warm, accessible, and completely free of jargon.

Whether she's teaching a seminary class, leading a Shabbat retreat, or chatting over coffee, Judy brings the same energy — curiosity, humor, and a genuine belief that these words have something urgent to say to each of us, personally.
